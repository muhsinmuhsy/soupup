About Us

Learn More About Us

Decades ago, this inspiring success story began when a group of youngsters decided to start a healthy food business. The idea of creating a soup restaurant offering a unique menu made with fresh, carefully selected globally, ingredients that were different from what people were used to, was an important pillar to ensure the success of the project. The idea of preparing each meal right in front of the customer's eyes was also the key to Soupup's success. Founders of Soupup have been able to create a new and unique dining experience in Kuwait, Saudi Arabia and India. We can say with great pride that we have been able to create a special concept that has transformed into a success.

Soupup is a progressive organisation specialising in the development of quality dining experiences In Kuwait, Saudi Arabia and India. At Soupup, we offer training courses for our employees to ensure that they provide the best service and the highest quality to our customers. We also strive to instill our values in all our employees at all levels. We have been able to achieve the highest standards of quality and efficiency through intensive training programs for employees and implementing management systems at the highest level to understand all the needs of customers and cater to their expectations.

Soupup is also proud to be one of the fully Halal international quick service restaurants (QSR) brands. We serve fresh, great tasting food, using only the highest quality herbs and spices. What Makes It Awesome One of the best stand-alone restaurants to bring unique cuisine In the city, Soupup has been going strong for years now. It is as Insta-worthy as it can get
